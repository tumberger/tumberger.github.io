About Me

I am currently a research intern at a16z Crypto. My primary interest lies at the intersection of cryptography and computer security. My current focus is on applications of cryptographic proof systems and secure computation. Previously, I was pursuing my PhD as a Research Assistant at Technical Univeristy of Munich, under the supervision of Prof. Sebastian Steinhorst and Dr. Arthur Gervais.

In 2023, I was as a Visiting Researcher at the Simons Institute for the Theory of Computing. In 2022, I was a Visiting Researcher at University of California, Berkeley under the supervision of Prof. Dawn Song.

For more details regarding my current affiliation, publications and talks please take a look at my CV (Updated 04/24).
